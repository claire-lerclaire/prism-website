FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who can apply as a delegate? +
Anyone who is passionate about global issues and wants to engage in high-level discussions. Seats are limited and admission is selective.
Is this an exclusive conference? +
Yes. All applications are personally reviewed by the Prism team to ensure the quality and authenticity of each delegate. Submitting a form does not guarantee a seat. Successful applicants will be notified via email.
What programme types are available? +
Prism 2026 offers two programme types. Standard includes the full conference programme but does not include accommodation — delegates arrange their own stay. All-Inclusive includes both the conference programme and accommodation throughout the event. Select your preferred option in the application form.
What is the conference language? +
All proceedings are conducted in English. Delegates are expected to communicate, debate, and write position papers and resolutions in English.
When will exact dates be confirmed? +
Exact conference dates will be announced soon. Registered applicants will be notified immediately via email. Check this page for updates.
What should I write in my motivation letter? +
Your essay should explain why you want to join Prism GYC, which global issues you care most about, what committee topics interest you, and what unique perspective you bring. Aim for 200–400 words.
